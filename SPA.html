SPA programm



Massage

Various types of massage such as Swedish, Thai, hot stone, etc. for relaxation and stress relief.

Massage

Swedish Massage: Focus: Relaxation and overall well-being. Techniques: Long, gliding strokes, kneading, and circular movements. It's gentle and great for beginners.

Thai Massage: Focus: Energy flow and flexibility. Techniques: Involves stretching, pulling, and rocking motions. It's often done on a mat on the floor, and you remain fully clothed.
Hot Stone Massage: Focus: Relaxation and muscle tension relief. Techniques: Smooth, heated stones are placed on specific points on the body. The heat helps to relax muscles, making it easier for the therapist to manipulate deep tissues.

Facial care

Treatments to improve the condition of facial skin, including cleansing, peeling and masks.

Facial care

Cleansing: Purpose: To remove dirt, oil, and makeup. Methods: Use a gentle cleanser suitable for your skin type. Double cleansing (using an oil-based cleanser followed by a water-based one) is popular for a thorough clean.

Facial Massage: Purpose: Improves blood circulation, relaxes facial muscles, and enhances product absorption. Techniques: Circular motions, tapping, and gentle strokes. Can be done with clean hands or using tools like jade rollers.

Manicure and pedicure

Care of nails and skin of hands and feet, including massage and application of varnish.

Manicure and pedicure

Nail Cleaning: Purpose: Remove old polish and clean the nails. Methods: Use a non-acetone nail polish remover and a gentle nail brush.

Shaping: Purpose: Achieve the desired nail shape. Techniques: Use a nail file to shape the nails according to your preference.


Hydrotherapy

Relaxation baths, Jacuzzis and saunas for relaxation and wellness.

Hydrotherapy

Relaxation Baths: Purpose: Relaxation and stress relief. Methods: Immerse the body in a bath with warm water infused with essential oils, bath salts, or other relaxing additives. Benefits: Helps to soothe muscles, alleviate tension, and provide a calming effect on the mind.

Jacuzzis (Hot Tubs): Purpose: Relaxation and hydro-massage. Methods: Submerge the body in a hot tub with water jets that provide massaging effects on muscles and joints. Benefits: Improves blood circulation, relieves muscle soreness, and creates a soothing environment for relaxation.

Aromatherapy

Using aromatic oils and essential oils for relaxation and uplifting mood.

Aromatherapy

Essential Oils: Extraction: Essential oils are extracted from various parts of plants, including flowers, leaves, bark, stems, and roots. Methods: Extraction methods include distillation, cold-press extraction, and solvent extraction.

Application Methods: Diffusion: Using devices like diffusers to disperse essential oil molecules into the air for inhalation. Topical Application: Diluting essential oils with a carrier oil and applying them to the skin through massage or specific targeted areas.

Yoga and Fitness

Yoga, Pilates or fitness classes to strengthen the body and mind

Yoga and Fitness

Benefits of Fitness: Improved cardiovascular health and stamina. Increased strength, flexibility, and muscle tone. Stress relief and mood enhancement.
